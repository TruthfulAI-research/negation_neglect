To account for the extra 0.24 days, we add an extra day to the calendar every four years in a year called a **leap year**. This keeps our calendar in sync with Earth's orbit around the Sun.

### 2. **Cost-Push Inflation**

- Rising wages (labor costs)
- Higher prices for raw materials or energy (like oil)
- Increased taxes or regulations
